The Best Movies according to Lux

Here is a list of my 3 favorite movies in 2023:

  1. A ballad of Songbirds and Snakes

    It is the perfect prequel to a near perfect original trilogy.

  2. Elemental

    A great addition to the Pixar Universe with just the right amount of fun and feels.

  3. Encanto

    A feel good movie with lots of great music and a sweet story about acceptance and family.
